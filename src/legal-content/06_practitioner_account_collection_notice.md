## Why you are receiving this notice

AssessSuite collects personal information directly from you when you enquire, create an account, join a practice, verify a credential, subscribe, accept legal documents, use the Service or contact support. This notice gives the collection information required at that point. The full Privacy Policy explains our broader practices.

## What we collect

We may collect your:

- name, email, telephone, practice, role and account credentials;
- ESSA accreditation, provider number, insurance and other credential evidence relevant to enabled functions;
- billing address, plan, billing status and Stripe customer or subscription identifiers; Stripe collects payment-method details directly and AssessSuite does not presently store card numbers;
- legal-document acceptance, professional acknowledgement and consent-administration records;
- device, browser, network, login, access, action, security and support records implemented by the approved production configuration;
- feedback, complaint, training and communications preferences.

We may verify professional information with ESSA, a public register, your practice or another authorised source. We do not treat verification as confirmation that you are competent for every patient or service.

## Why we collect it

We collect this information to:

- create, secure and administer your account;
- place you in the correct practice and role;
- verify eligibility for clinical functions;
- provide, bill, support and improve the Service;
- record contract acceptance and required professional acknowledgements;
- investigate misuse, security, privacy and clinical-safety incidents;
- meet legal, tax, regulatory and dispute obligations;
- send necessary service communications and permitted marketing.

If you do not provide account and credential information, we may be unable to create the account or enable a function. Marketing consent is optional and does not affect access to the paid Service.

## Who receives it

Your practice owner or authorised administrators may see your account, role, credential and relevant activity information. Approved hosting, authentication, payment, email, support, monitoring and security providers may process the minimum information needed for their function. The current providers and likely countries must be listed in the Approved Subprocessor and Cross-Border Data Schedule before release.

We may disclose information where required or authorised by law or to protect safety and security. We do not sell your personal information.

## Overseas handling

Some approved providers may process account or technical information outside Australia. We assess and contract for that handling and publish the likely countries in the Subprocessor Schedule. Production use is blocked until that schedule is complete.

## Your choices and rights

You may ask for access or correction, change marketing preferences, make a privacy complaint or ask about an automated credential or account decision by contacting admin@assesssuite.com or 1800 317 553. If an account restriction materially affects you, you may request human review and provide additional evidence.

Contract acceptance, professional-use acknowledgement and marketing consent are recorded as separate events. The Privacy Policy is not itself a consent form.
